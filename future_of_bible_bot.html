Understanding the Token-Based System:

Bible Bot operates on a token-based system, a method where each interaction with the bot consumes a certain number of "tokens." Think of tokens as units of computation that translate into a cost. This transparent and straightforward approach ensures that you know exactly what you're paying for. Here's how it breaks down:

  • Input: $0.03 / 1K tokens
  • Output: $0.06 / 1K tokens
  • Average Tokens per Question: 500
  • Average Questions per User (Per Day): 5
  • Average Cost Per User (Per Day): $0.45
  • Total Daily Cost for 10,000 Users: $4,500

This pricing model ensures accessibility and transparency, without compromising quality or depth.
