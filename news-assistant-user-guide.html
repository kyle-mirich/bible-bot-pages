How to Customize Your News Gathering

1. Gathering Top News

  • Interval: Set how frequently you want the tool to gather top news. Times are in minutes.
  • Max Results: Specify the maximum number of top news articles you'd like to see each time the app gathers data.

2. Keyword-Based Data Gathering for Your Brand

  • Interval: Choose how often you want the app to look for articles containing specific keywords, such as your brand name.
  • Max Results: Select the maximum number of articles to gather.
  • Keywords: Enter the relevant keywords, separated by commas.

Click the "Update Keyword Settings" button to save your preferences.

3. Gathering Industry-Specific News

  • Interval: Select the frequency for industry news updates.
  • Max Results: Limit the number of articles related to your industry.
  • Topic: Choose an industry topic from the dropdown menu.

Press "Update Topic Settings" to save.

4. Gathering Location-Specific News

  • Interval: Set how frequently you want news from specific locations.
  • Max Results: Limit the number of articles based on location.
  • Locations: Enter the locations you're interested in, separated by commas.

Click "Update Location Settings" to save.

Taking Actions

  • Start: Click this to initiate news gathering based on your settings.
  • Stop: Click to halt the news gathering.

Downloading News Articles

Sidebar: File Explorer

  • Select a Folder: Navigate to "File Explorer" in the sidebar. Use the dropdown menu to pick a folder where your gathered articles will be stored.
  • Choose an Article: After selecting a folder, you'll see a list of articles in .docx format. Pick any article you'd like to download.
  • Download Options:
    • Download Selected .docx: Click to download the individual article.
    • Download All .docx in Selected Folder as Zip: Click to download all articles in the folder as a zip file.

Housekeeping: Reset Data Folder

Should you wish to clean the slate and start fresh, there's a "Reset Data Folder" button that will remove all gathered articles, allowing you to start anew.
